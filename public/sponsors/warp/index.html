VII · The Warp case Episode 238

When a sponsor's name is in the title, the reach you can't count is even bigger.

Episode 238, “Next.js is dead, long live Next.js,” was the top-performing recap on nerdy.dev this quarter — presented by Warp.

Every aggregator, screenshot, quote-post, and newsletter that repeated the title carried a sponsor's brand with it. Those impressions are untrackable — and almost certainly larger than the ones on this page.
